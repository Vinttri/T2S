Business Knowledge & Specifications

This file holds database-specific business knowledge — the domain concepts,
metric definitions, naming conventions, and term-to-data mappings that are
particular to one connected database and are not expressible as general
SQL-craft rules.

It is intentionally empty by default. Supply the definitions for your own
database here, or load them per-database through the document loader. The
schema itself — table and column names, descriptions, declared relationships,
keys, and sample values — together with the general rules in `user_rules.md`
remains authoritative; this file only adds concepts that supplement them.
